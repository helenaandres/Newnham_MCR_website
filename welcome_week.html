Welcome Week

If you are joining Newnham as a graduate student this coming autumn, welcome! This website is about the MCR (Middle Common Room) which is the community of graduate students at Newnham. The MCR has a committee whose roles are to represent you, the student body. We will be sending out all the information you need about arriving at Newnham closer to the start of term. To keep up to date with information, please join the Newnham MCR Freshers Facebook for the 2018-19 intake.

You can also join the main Newnham MCR Facebook page.

And we have a page specifically to promote social events.

We also have an instagram page which you can follow to get daily doses of Newnham before you arrive! Just search Newnham MCR on Instagram.

During the first few weeks of term the Newnham MCR committee will be holding a wide range of events to help you settle into life at Cambridge and get familiar with Newnham. The schedule will be put up as soon as everything has been finalised, but it is likely to include events like guided tours of college and Cambridge city centre, trips to places like Grantchester, the Botanic Gardens and Fitzwilliam Museum, film nights, quiz nights and a Champagne Brunch. Lots of opportunity for you get get to know your fellow Newnhamites.

If you have any questions at all about coming to Newnham/starting life as a graduate student at Cambridge, please ask away in the Facebook group, or email either the MCR President (Sam Leggett ) or Secretary (Josie Gaynord). We will be more than happy to answer your questions! There is also information on the main Newnham College website about graduate life at Newnham, and contact details for relevant college members of staff.
